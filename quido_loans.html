Payment holiday
Pause your payment for a month

A payment holiday pauses your next payment. Interest continues to accrue and a new instalment is added to the end of your loan.

Are you in short-term or long-term financial difficulty?

Based on your situation, a payment holiday may not be enough. Our team can discuss a more tailored solution with you.

We recommend speaking with us so we can find the best option for your circumstances.

Interest will continue to accrue during your holiday, increasing the total you repay.
