Account Actions

All actions are logged and synced to the customer account in real time

Account Status
Change the account status. Affects available options for the customer and collections workflow.
Payment Holiday
Pause the next instalment. Interest accrues and is added to the outstanding balance. Max 2 per loan.
Record Manual Payment
Record a payment received outside the automated system (cash, BACS, etc.).
Change Pay Date
Shift all future due dates to a different day of the month. Use 1–28 to avoid month-end issues.
Payment Arrangement
Restructure remaining balance into reduced payments with frozen interest for customers in financial difficulty.
Extend Loan Term
Add months to the loan term, reducing the monthly EMI and easing the customer’s repayment burden.
Waive Interest
Write off a portion of accrued interest. Used for goodwill gestures or financial hardship cases.
Flag for Collections
Escalate this account to the collections team. A contact log entry is required before escalating.
Close Account
Permanently close and terminate the account. This action is irreversible and fully logged.
